What is SUSTAIN(A)GAME?

A digital serious game that helps players explore climate, circular economy, and life cycle impacts through interactive, replayable scenarios. Built as a product-based platform: create once, scale globally.

Game-first
Strategy, not slides

Players manage resources, infrastructure and behaviors, instead of reading long reports. Learning emerges from meaningful decisions and consequences.

LCA-inspired
Impact you can see

Choices trigger real-time changes in footprints: CO₂, water, materials and circularity scores, based on simplified LCA logic.

Scalable
Global digital reach

Mobile and browser-based, with low marginal cost per player. Suitable for individual learners and large cohorts around the world.

How the game works

Each scenario turns complex sustainability challenges into a clear, repeatable decision loop that invites experimentation and systems thinking.

1. Choose a scenario
From Circular City to Zero-Waste Campus and Sustainable Supply Chain, players select the context that fits their interests.
2. Make strategic decisions
Allocate budgets, select technologies, design policies and behaviors — balancing quality of life with environmental constraints.
3. Get instant LCA-style feedback
See how your decisions affect CO₂, water, material use and circularity across the full life cycle.
4. Optimize, compare & replay
Compete with yourself and others, try new strategies, and discover sustainable pathways through play.
